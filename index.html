Joe Deegan

I am an Irish doctor working as a specialty registrar in public health medicine in the UK.

I’m interested in evaluation, operational research, and design thinking in healthcare. I completed my medical degree in Trinity College Dublin, and my MSc in Public Health at the London School of Hygiene and Tropical Medicine.

I’m currently working as part of the public health team at Kent County Council.

Before this, I worked in acute medicine in St James’s Hospital, Dublin. I was the lead NCHD at St James’s Hospital in 2022-2023, and received a George Moore Scholarship from the Ireland’s Funds to study public health in London.

If you’d like to chat to me about my work or anything at all, please get in touch! You can find me on LinkedIn or Bluesky.
